Title: We are oblivious of the inflation seeping underground.


Looking at Graph 1, inflation is not expected in the US, the UK, and the EA. Moving to the
much shorter-term interest rates in Graphs 2 & 3, the market does not expect the rate rise in
the near future. The media and the governments are also keeping the nose on the downside.
However, Graphs 4 and 5 show S&P 500 is touching 2,100, and Brent is approaching $45. The
inflation is seeping through under the ground of the US economy. It is just invisible to the naked
eyes. The inflation of the US was 0.9% in March in Graph 6, and it is en route for the UK and the EA.
If Americans want to make money, buy stocks in the stock market, oil in the futures market, and
push Donald Trump into the White House. Market will always move one way. You can sleep well
at every night.
